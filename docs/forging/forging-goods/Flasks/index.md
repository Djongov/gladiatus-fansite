Flasks are important liquid materials used in various prefixes and suffixes. They include potions, tinctures, essences, and other alchemical ingredients essential for crafting powerful items.

---

## Common Flasks

- [Scorpion Poison](scorpion-poison) - Common material for low level suffixes
- [Tincture of Stamina](tincture-of-stamina) - Important for high level prefixes like Lucius
- [Antidote](antidote) - Common material for low-mid level suffixes
- [Tincture of Enlightenment](tincture-of-enlightenment) - Common for low-mid level healing suffixes

---

## Medium Rarity Flasks

- [Potion of Perception](potion-of-perception) - Important for mid-high level prefixes
- [Essence of Reaction](essence-of-reaction) - Material for mid-high level suffixes
- [Phial of Charisma](phial-of-charisma) - Rare/Medium material for mid-high level suffixes

---

## Rare Flasks

- [Adrenaline](adrenaline) - Rare material for few mid-level prefixes
- [Soul Essence](soul-essence) - Rare material for very high level prefixes
- [Waters of Oblivion](waters-of-oblivion) - Rare material for very high level suffixes
