An important rune that is part of a lot of good mid-high level prefixes. It's not rare but used a lot so players may always be feeling like they don't have enough.

---

## Drops From

- Drops 45% from Dragon Whelp in Koman Mountains
- Drops 45% from Skeleton Berserker in Vandal Village
- Drops 25% from Daug in Mine
