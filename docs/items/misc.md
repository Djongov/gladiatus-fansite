## Misc
---------------------

Misc items are items that do not fall into any category or are currently with no wide-spread or known use

    **Godly Oils**

These oils are given when the 2nd godly prayer is consumed. Each god gives a different oil which lasts 3 days (or more if boosted by costume)

![Godly Oil](https://gladiatusfansite.blob.core.windows.net/images/Startup_guide/godly_oils_example.png "Godly Oil")

    **Holy Oil of Rage**

![Holy Oil of Rage](https://gladiatusfansite.blob.core.windows.net/images/Items/img/Misc/Holy_Oil_of_Rage.png "Holy Oil of Rage")

![Holy Oil of Rage](https://gladiatusfansite.blob.core.windows.net/images/Items/img/Misc/Holy_Oil_of_Rage_stats.png "Holy Oil of Rage")

Source: Apollo
