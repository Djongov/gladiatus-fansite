Item Prefixes
=============

From prefixes and suffixes, Prefixes are the driving and influential part of forming an item. There are a lot of Prefixes that are defining in the game and will be dominating the item landscape.

**Important! If you need a certain material (forging good) and you are looking for a suitable prefix or suffix to smelt in order to get as many as you can - Use the [Materials](Materials "Materials") page instead. Under every material i have the prefixes/suffixes that smelts the most of this material**

I already have contributors that helped with some of the prefixes! Thanks Jimmy West, Miroslav Petrov, Pesho69, DirtyWorkz. Also a huge donation of UW-only high level rpefixes from Tri Lê and \[Terror\]Teufel\* , Balkan\_Devil, Spectrell from DE201
