## Reinforcements
-----------------------------------------

Reinforcements are temporary buffs to an item that will increase a certain stat. They only last a limited amount of time and are not permanent. Reinforcements boost the character stats, health points and damage. Typical for reinforcements and upgrades is that every color boosts a certain character stat. Here is the order:

Blue - Strength

Yellow - Dexterity

Green - Agility

Orange - Constitution

Purple - Charisma

Red - Intelligence

Just like any other item, reinforcements can be different quality colors and the higher the color the more stats it buffs. Also the item level of the item is contributing to how much stat it boosts. Reinforcements can be dropped from mobs or purchased from the Alchemist vendor or the Auction house under Reinforcements.
