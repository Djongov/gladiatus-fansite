## Free Ways to Obtain Rubies

However, ![Rubies](https://gladiatusfansite.blob.core.windows.net/images/icon_rubies.gif "Rubies") can also be obtained without paying in few ways:

### Drop from Mobs

Very low drop % chance from mobs in the game (Expeditions, Dungeons, Events, Underworld). Here is [evidence](https://gladiatusfansite.blob.core.windows.net/images/Startup_guide/Ruby_drop.png "Ruby drop"). 

There isn't an exact % drop chance but it is extremely low. Can be boosted by Micro Events or costumes.

### Daily Login Bonuses

From the daily login bonuses. Here is [evidence](https://gladiatusfansite.blob.core.windows.net/images/Startup_guide/Ruby_daily.png "Ruby drop"). 

1-2 rubies for a daily bonus and if Rubies are awarded from the bonus weekly it could be 4-6 Rubies. Here is [evidence](https://gladiatusfansite.blob.core.windows.net/images/Startup_guide/Ruby_weekly_bonus.png "evidence").

### Event Bonuses

- Some Micro [Events](/events) are buffing the entire server and raising the Ruby drop % from expeditions
- [Ra's Light Robe](/events/event-costumes) event costume increases Ruby drop from expeditions by 8%
- Event items - [Special Food](/items#Special-Food) or [Event Items](/items#Event) can award Rubies or Centurio

## Where to Spend Rubies

Rubies can be used in various ways:

- **Reduce cooldowns** in Expeditions, Dungeons, Forging
- **Recharge Event expedition points**
- **Purchase [Centurion](centurion.md) subscription** (15 rubies for 14 days)
- **Activate [Pacts](pacts.md)** (15 rubies per pact for 14 days)
- **Buy premium items** from [Treasury](/game-guide/pantheon/treasury)
- **Purchase extra bag slots** (7 rubies for bags V-VI, 14 rubies for bags VII-VIII)

Rubies can be used in various other ways but will also be discussed in other sections of the guide.
