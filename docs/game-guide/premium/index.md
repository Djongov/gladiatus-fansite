## [Rubies](rubies.md)

Rubies ![Rubies](https://gladiatusfansite.blob.core.windows.net/images/icon_rubies.gif "Rubies") are the premium currency in Gladiatus. They can be purchased from the Jeweler for real money or obtained for free through various in-game methods.

**Ways to Obtain Rubies:**
- Purchase from the Jeweler (with occasional discounts)
- Very low drop chance from mobs (Expeditions, Dungeons, Events, Underworld)
- Daily login bonuses
- Event costumes that boost ruby drops
- Special event items

**Where to Spend Rubies:**
- Reduce cooldowns (Expeditions, Dungeons, Forging)
- Purchase Centurion subscription
- Buy premium items from Treasury
- Activate Pacts

[Read more about Rubies →](rubies.md)

## [Centurion](centurion.md)

Centurion is the premium subscription feature in Gladiatus. It provides significant benefits for both you and your guild.

**Key Benefits:**
- Receive half of stable boy's daily wages in gold
- 100% Healing Potion
- 3% discount on guild buildings per active Centurion (max 30%)
- 5 minute battle block in arena (instead of 15 minutes)
- Maximum expedition points increased to 24
- Maximum dungeon points increased to 24
- Halved expedition and dungeon duration
- Increased point regeneration

**Cost:** 15 ![Rubies](https://gladiatusfansite.blob.core.windows.net/images/icon_rubies.gif "Rubies") for 14 days

[Read more about Centurion →](centurion.md)

## [Pacts](pacts.md)

Pacts are one of the most fundamental premium features of the game. They provide powerful bonuses across different aspects of gameplay.

**4 Pact Categories:**

1. **Blessing Pacts** - Focused on expeditions and regeneration
2. **Seal Pacts** - Helping with items, market, and vendors
3. **Honours Pacts** - Combat capabilities and stats
4. **Secret Knowledge Pacts** - Items, quests, and learning bonuses

Each pact costs 15 ![Rubies](https://gladiatusfansite.blob.core.windows.net/images/icon_rubies.gif "Rubies") for 14 days or can be activated with tokens from the Chest of Divine Fate. You can switch between pacts within the same category once per day (24-hour cooldown).

[Read more about Pacts →](pacts.md)
