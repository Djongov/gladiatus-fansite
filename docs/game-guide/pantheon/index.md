## [Quests](quests.md)

Quests are a fundamental part of the game. They can be with time limits or without expiration dates, and provide rewards such as God favors, Gold, Experience, Honour, and Items.

There are 6 categories of quests:
- Combat Quests
- Expedition Quests
- Dungeon Quests
- Arena Quests
- Circus Quests
- Item Quests
- Work Quests

[Read more about Quests →](quests.md)

## [Missions](missions.md)

Missions are one-time achievements that your character can achieve and be rewarded. Completing missions increases your total (max) favor with the gods!

Mission categories include:
- Character Development
- Expeditions
- Arenas
- Equipment
- Miscellaneous

[Read more about Missions →](missions.md)

---

## [Gods](gods.md)

The Gods system plays a significant role in Gladiatus. There are 6 Gods: Mars, Mercury, Diana, Apollo, Minerva, and Vulcan. Gather favor with each god and spend it to activate powerful buffs.

Each god offers 3 ranks of bonuses:
- Rank 1: Gold and Experience bonuses
- Rank 2: Holy Oils for stat boosts
- Rank 3: Special combat effects

[Read more about Gods →](gods.md)

---

## [Treasury](treasury.md)

Treasury is the home of the Chest of Divine Fate, which contains 12 possible treasures of 3 different quality levels. Open chests to win premium items, pacts, consumables, and rare rewards.

Notable rewards include:
- Mobilisation (restores expedition points)
- Gate Keys (restores dungeon points)
- Magical Bags (extra inventory slots)
- Holy Hourglasses (skip cooldowns)
- Pacts and premium items

[Read more about Treasury →](treasury.md)
