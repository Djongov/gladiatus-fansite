Italy expeditions are the first one you encounter in the game. They include mostly low level expeditions and a couple of higher level ones. The stats on all enemies are without any bonuses.

![Italy Expedition](https://gladiatusfansite.blob.core.windows.net/images/Expeditions/Italy/Italy_Overview.png "Italy Expedition")

![Italy Town](https://gladiatusfansite.blob.core.windows.net/images/Expeditions/Italy/Italy_Town.png "Italy Town")

## Expeditions

- **[Grimwood](/expeditions/italy-expeditions/grimwood "Grimwood")** - Entry Level 1
- **[Pirate Harbour](/expeditions/italy-expeditions/pirate-harbour "Pirate Harbour")** - Entry Level 5
- **[Misty Mountains](/expeditions/italy-expeditions/misty-mountains "Misty Mountains")** - Entry Level 10
- **[Wolf Cave](/expeditions/italy-expeditions/wolf-cave "Wolf Cave")** - Entry Level 15
- **[Ancient Temple](/expeditions/italy-expeditions/ancient-temple "Ancient Temple")** - Entry Level 60
- **[Barbarian Village](/expeditions/italy-expeditions/barbarian-village "Barbarian Village")** - Entry Level 65
- **[Bandit Camp](/expeditions/italy-expeditions/bandit-camp "Bandit Camp")** - Entry Level 70
