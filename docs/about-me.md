I am Dimitar and I am from Bulgaria. I play Gladiatus since 2010 and even though I no longer play actively, I love the game and try to be actively involved in its community. This fansite was first developed in 2016 as a Joomla 3 CMS template but now in 2026, it is being re-written in Docusaurus and made public in [Github](https://github.com/Djongov/gladiatus-fansite "Github")
