# Character Planner

This is early BETA

<CharacterPlanner />

## Features

- **Visual Equipment Slots**: Click on any equipment slot to add items
- **Item Customization**: Choose base items, prefixes, suffixes, rarity, and conditioning
- **Real-time Stats**: See your total character stats update instantly
- **Shareable Builds**: Generate a URL to share your character build
- **Accurate Calculations**: Uses authentic Gladiatus formulas for damage, armor, and stats

## How to Use

1. **Click Equipment Slots**: Click on any of the 9 equipment slots on the character doll
2. **Select Items**: Browse and search for items to equip
3. **Customize**: Add prefixes, suffixes, choose rarity, and enable conditioning
4. **View Stats**: See your total character stats in the stats panel
5. **Share**: Click "Share Build" to copy a shareable URL to your clipboard

## Equipment Slots

- **Helmet**: Head protection
- **Amulet**: Neck accessory
- **Chest**: Main armor
- **Gloves**: Hand protection
- **Main Hand**: Primary weapon or shield
- **Off Hand**: Secondary weapon or shield
- **Ring 1 & 2**: Finger accessories
- **Shoes**: Foot protection

## Tips

- Items can have different rarities: Common, Green (Ceres), Blue (Neptune), Purple (Mars), Orange (Jupiter), and Red (Olympus)
- Conditioning (+) adds an extra rarity tier of stats
- Prefixes and suffixes add levels and special bonuses to items
- Weapons in main hand and off hand both contribute to total damage
- All armor pieces contribute to your total armor value

## Sharing Builds

When you click "Share Build", a URL is generated with your current equipment encoded in it. Anyone with this URL can load your exact character build and see the same stats.

The URL includes:
- All equipped items
- Prefixes and suffixes
- Rarity levels
- Conditioning status
